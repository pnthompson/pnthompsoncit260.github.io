A home town like no other...

Preston Idaho isn't just about Napoleon Dynamite or taking your bike off of "Sweet Jumps." There is a lot of very interesting history in and around the Preston Idaho area. One of the biggest attractions to this area is the several natural Hotsprings. People have come from all over to experience the naturally heated pools, loaded with minerals that supposedly help what ails your aches and pains. If that isn't your thing, Try the Preston Rodeo, which is one of the biggest in this part of the Country. The Preston Rodeo draws cowfolk from all over the world to compete in various events. During Christmas downtown Preston is a glow with lights. They even have a park that is completely covered in lights. It has easy driving access so you don't even need to leave the comfort of your vehicle to enjoy the season. FUN fact Preston is even home to a burger king that is said to be owned by the Utah Jazz NBA legend Karl Malone. It's said that He bought a few of the restaurants, the Preston Burger King is just one of them. There is far more to Preston than just a small town in Mid Western United States, It is a Gem in the Gem State, a real treasure. There are endless outdooor activities that can be enjoyed in this small little town. So stop by, partake in the wonder that is Preston, Who knows? you may even say its pretty "flippin sweet"
