Typography

There are 3 different font types:

  • "Shadows into light" - Header H2
  • "Passion One" - Header H1
  • "Helvetica"- Body

    • Cursive fonts are used in the headings to make the site more amiable. "Passive One" is a bold yet easily read font. "Shadows into light" is a more fun and personal font. It looks as though it was written in. While Helvetica is used in the body to ensure readability. If the information can't be easily read, users will turn elsewhere for their weather information.
