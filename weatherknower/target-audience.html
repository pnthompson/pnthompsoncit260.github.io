Target-Audience

Weather effects everyone. Whether they work outside, travel or just want to know what to expect. This site strives to reach the most people possible. Now with that said, This is a website, so that means the users will need to have access to the internet. The majority of users will probably use phones or tablets to access the site on the go. Most users will be over the age of 12, probably between 16 and 65 years of age.
